# Play Store Listing — Digital Detox

## App Name
Digital Detox - Screen Time

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Break the scroll habit. A mindful pause before every distracting app opens.

## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Do you open YouTube or Instagram without thinking? You're not alone. The average person picks up their phone 96 times a day — most of those are unconscious habits.

Digital Detox doesn't block your apps. It does something more powerful: it makes you pause and think before you scroll.

HOW IT WORKS
When you open a monitored app (YouTube, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, or any app you choose), Digital Detox shows a full-screen pause with:

- Your personal message to yourself — words that only YOU wrote, reminding YOU why you want to change
- A breathing animation to slow you down
- Your real usage stats: how many times you've opened this app today and how much time you've spent
- A mandatory cooldown timer before you can continue

After the pause, you choose: Go Back or Continue. If you continue, a floating countdown timer shows exactly when the next nudge will appear.

KEY FEATURES

Mindful Intervention
Every app open triggers a conscious pause. Not a block — a mirror.

Your Own Words
Write personal messages that appear on the intervention screen. Generic "are you sure?" messages get ignored. Your own words hit different.

Live Usage Stats
See exactly how many times you opened each app and how much time you spent — today and this week.

Smart Escalation
The more you open an app, the longer the pause gets. 1-3 opens = 8 seconds. 4-7 = 15 seconds. 8+ = 30 seconds.

Late Night Mode
Automatically doubles all cooldown timers between 10 PM and 6 AM — when mindless scrolling does the most damage.

Browser Protection
Monitors YouTube, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn even when accessed through Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or other browsers.

Floating Countdown Timer
After pressing Continue, a small countdown appears showing exactly when the next nudge will interrupt you.

Session Nudge
If you keep using a blocked app, the intervention re-appears after your configured time (1-60 minutes).

Dashboard
Beautiful weekly charts showing your screen time, opens, and mindful exits.

100% PRIVATE
All data stays on your device. No accounts. No servers. No analytics. No ads. Open source.

PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED
- Accessibility Service: Detects when monitored apps open
- Display Over Other Apps: Shows the pause screen
- Usage Access: Powers the dashboard stats
