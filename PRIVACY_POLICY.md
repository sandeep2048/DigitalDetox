# Privacy Policy for Digital Detox

**Last updated:** March 29, 2026

## Overview

Digital Detox is a screen time awareness app that helps you be mindful about your phone usage. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data Collection

### What we collect
- **App usage logs**: Which monitored apps you opened, how many times, and for how long. This data is used solely to display your usage statistics on the dashboard.
- **Custom messages**: The personal messages you write to yourself within the app.
- **App preferences**: Your settings (cooldown duration, night mode, selected apps).

### What we do NOT collect
- We do **not** collect personal information (name, email, phone number).
- We do **not** collect browsing history or URL content. The accessibility service only checks if a URL matches a monitored domain (e.g., youtube.com) and does not store or transmit the URL.
- We do **not** transmit any data to external servers. All data stays on your device.
- We do **not** use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
- We do **not** collect data from other apps. The accessibility service only detects which app is in the foreground.

## Permissions

### Accessibility Service
Used to detect when you open a monitored app so the mindful pause screen can be displayed. The service only reads the foreground app's package name and, for browsers, checks if the URL bar contains a monitored domain. No other content is accessed or stored.

### Display Over Other Apps
Used to show the intervention screen on top of monitored apps.

### Usage Access
Used to display your app usage statistics on the dashboard.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted Room database and Android DataStore. No data leaves your device. If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted.

## Third-Party Services

Digital Detox does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. There is no server component.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
